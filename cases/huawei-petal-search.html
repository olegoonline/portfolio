Petal Search — Huawei RU

// BRIEF

Petal Search is Huawei's native search platform — a search engine, marketplace, and local discovery product. In Russia it needed to feel genuinely local, not a translated global template. The Russian Petal Search Lab was a dedicated localisation team inside Huawei, coordinating with HQ (Singapore) and cross-country product teams in China, India, Turkey, and Mexico.

My role was to lead UX design for the Russian market — own feature quality, drive the marketplace team's UX direction, and ensure everything passed HQ review.

Platform

// APPROACH

The key insight was that Russian users search differently — maps-heavy, POI-heavy, event-driven. The global SERP template wasn't built for that pattern. The most impactful piece was the Nearby feature.

  • Led UX design at Petal Search Lab — drove features that passed HQ review faster than other offices.
  • Key delivery: launched the Russian 'Nearby' feature (maps, places, events, POIs) — praised by HQ for quality and metrics, delivered on schedule.
  • Led design for the marketplace team (10–20 people) — updated UX/UI for product collection pages and trading platform integrations, ran quick concept tests and campaign work.
  • Developed features collaboratively with product managers using Axure RP — user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes for search, discovery, and marketplace.
