Petal Search — Huawei RU

Led UX at Huawei's dedicated Russian-market localisation team inside Petal Search. Shipped the Nearby feature — maps, places, events — praised by HQ for quality and metrics. All stakeholder comms in English with Singapore.

Android · HarmonyOS / Team Lead / Senior UI-UX Designer / Feb '22 — Jun '22

about

Petal Search is Huawei's native search platform — search engine, marketplace, and local discovery. In Russia it needed to feel genuinely local, not a translated global template.

The Russian Petal Search Lab was a dedicated localisation team inside Huawei, coordinating with HQ in Singapore and product teams across China, India, Turkey, and Mexico.

challenges

Russian users search differently — maps-heavy, POI-heavy, event-driven. The global SERP template wasn't built for that pattern.

  • Led UX at Petal Search Lab — drove features that passed HQ review faster than other offices.
  • Key delivery: Russian 'Nearby' feature (maps, places, events, POIs) — praised by HQ for quality and metrics, delivered on schedule.
  • Led design for the marketplace team (10–20 people) — updated UX/UI for product collection pages and trading platform integrations.
  • Developed features with product managers using Axure RP — user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes for search, discovery, and marketplace.
