petal search lab / huawei

I worked as a Senior UI/UX Designer on the Russian version of the Huawei Petal Search engine. Our team focused on developing the mobile application and its market promotion.

Lead Product Designer
01

the product

We were responsible for adapting Petal Search for the Russian market. We had relative freedom and significant influence on decision-making regarding the development direction. Structurally, we were separated from R&D and operated as a development laboratory, even referred to as the Petal Search Lab.

02

my team

The team consisted of various specialists, including individuals from China who worked in the Russian office; some of them were part of our team. The main office immediately noticed that our work was of high quality, and the features quickly passed reviews.

03

partners

We collaborated with various trading platforms, providing their listings for sale through the Petal Search marketplace. I was responsible for updating the store's UX and UI, implementing modern practices, and analysing competitor decisions.

04

process

The marketplace was quite an interesting part of the job because it was very dynamic. We could quickly test various hypotheses, make quick adjustments, create campaigns, and so on.

05

design & nda

I can only show what's currently available in the public domain. As far as I understood, the project was discontinued after the reduction in R&D activities in 2022. There's an international version of Nearby, but it's not the same at all. We had a very cool adaptation specifically for Russia – with local theaters, movies, points of interest. One of the coolest among all the offices.
