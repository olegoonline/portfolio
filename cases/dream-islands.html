Dream Islands

A travel and wellness app concept — helping users find trips tailored to their health goals and recovery needs. Trip discovery paired with wellbeing tracking.

iOS, Mobile / Product Design

about

Dream Islands is a concept travel app that bridges trip planning with personal wellness. Users find destinations matched to their health goals — stress relief, recovery, performance — rather than browsing by destination alone.

The core loop: set a wellbeing goal, get curated trip recommendations, track your progress during and after the trip.

approach

The design challenge was making health-driven travel feel natural and motivating, not clinical. The interface needed to feel like a personal travel companion.

  • Trip cards lead with the goal and outcome, not just destination name and price.
  • Wellbeing score surfaces at-a-glance — users see their sleep, movement, and mindfulness data without switching context.
  • Filters like "Stress relief" and "Performance" make the health-first search model immediately understandable.
  • Visual language stays warm and nature-forward to reinforce the restorative intent.
