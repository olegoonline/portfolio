Last Level — Product

Product design and design leadership on a consumer product — from raw concept to shipping team. I owned the design end of the stack and grew the design function alongside it.

// BRIEF

Last Level was a consumer product with a clear north star but a fuzzy middle. The founding team had validated demand but the experience was held together with glue and the design org was a team of one.

I came in to own product design end-to-end while also building a design function that could outlast me.

Surface
Mobile-first web
Audience
B2C
Stage
Post-PMF, scaling
Status
Shipped

// APPROACH

Two tracks running in parallel — keep shipping while quietly rebuilding the foundations.

  • Mapped the existing product end-to-end and identified the three flows that drove 80% of revenue.
  • Reworked those flows with a small set of patterns we could reuse — fewer screens, sharper hierarchy, less prose.
  • Hired two designers, wrote the IC ladder, set up critique and design reviews from scratch.
  • Spun up a lightweight design system as a side-effect of shipping, not as a six-month project.
