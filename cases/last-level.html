Last Level — Modern Strike Online

Led the full UX/UI redesign of a live mobile FPS. Drove a hypothesis-driven store redesign that lifted conversion by 50%, and built the design department from scratch — 2 designers, 1 artist, 1 animator.

Mobile Game · iOS / Android / Team Lead / Senior UI-UX Designer / Jun '22 — Oct '24

about

Modern Strike Online is a live mobile first-person shooter with an active player base. I joined as the first design lead — the game had existing UX but no coherent design direction and no design team.

My mandate: own the full UX/UI direction, drive a complete redesign, and build a team capable of sustaining it long-term.

challenges

The biggest lever was the in-game store. With product and analytics, I led a hypothesis-driven redesign grounded in player data.

  • Led store redesign — qualitative interviews, funnel analysis, and beta-tester feedback to define and validate hypotheses.
  • +50% conversion into the store, ~40% reduction in purchase-flow friction, +5–20% lift in promoted-item purchases.
  • Built the design department from scratch: hired 2 designers, 1 UI artist, and 1 UI animator — recognised as the most process-disciplined team in the studio, never missed a deadline.
  • Made research and validation part of how the team worked — player surveys and funnel analysis went straight into roadmap priorities.
