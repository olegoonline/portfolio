// BRIEF

Modern Strike Online is a live mobile first-person shooter with an active player base. I joined as the first design lead — the game had existing UX but no coherent design direction, no design team, and metrics that pointed to friction in the store.

My mandate: own the full UX/UI direction, drive a complete redesign, and build a team capable of sustaining it long-term.

Product
Mobile FPS · Modern Strike Online
Platform

Stage
Live · iterating
Status
Shipped

// APPROACH

The biggest lever was the in-game store. With product and analytics, I led a hypothesis-driven redesign: qualitative interviews with players, funnel analysis, and feedback from beta testers. Every decision was tied to a specific friction point we'd identified in the data.

  • Led full redesign of the in-game store — qualitative interviews, funnel analysis, and player feedback to define hypotheses and measure results.
  • Built the design department from scratch: hired 2 designers, 1 UI artist, and 1 UI animator. Recognised as the most process-disciplined team in the studio — never missed a deadline.
  • Made research and validation part of how the team worked — player surveys, beta-tester feedback, and funnel reviews with analytics went straight into roadmap priorities.
  • Maintained the best documentation in the studio. Coordinated with project managers, in-house engineering, and third-party agencies on studio-wide projects and website redesign.
