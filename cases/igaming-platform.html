Multi-Brand B2B/B2C Platform

Built a design system that cut brand-kit delivery from a month to a week across 13 active brands. Led a team of 6, shipped back-office tooling for 50–100 operators, and put AI in the team's daily workflow.

Mobile-First / Lead Product Designer / Dec '24 — Present

about

A mobile-first platform running 13 active brands on a shared design system. Each brand needed its own identity while sharing the same component base.

I joined as Lead Product Designer to build UI/UX of the platform — both B2C and back-office. I built and led a team of 6 designers.

challenges

Built the system with an atomic architecture and design tokens — primitive → semantic → brand layers. Each brand became a configuration file on top of a shared component base.

  • Set sprint priorities, ran design reviews, mentored team on systems thinking and engineering handoff, set up onboarding for new designers.
  • Used Microsoft Clarity and internal metric sources for research, analytics and UX insights.
  • Designed end-to-end UX for a multi-role back-office used by nearly 100 operators: multi-state workflows, content tooling, role-based cabinets for ops, support, finance, and compliance.
  • Built internal AI tools on Claude for rapid prototyping — cut prototyping time on new flows by ~30% and made AI part of the team's daily workflow.
