b2c/b2b platform

A mobile-first platform running 13 active brands on a shared design system. Each brand needed its own identity while sharing the same component base.

I joined as Lead Product Designer to help create UI/UX of the platform from the ground up – both the B2C and B2B part. I built and lead the team of 6 designers.

Lead Product Designer
01

it started with vision

I designed in close collaboration with the CEO, and together we shaped a shared vision for the UI and UX. The result was a clean design with clear visual and interaction patterns, and one that's easy to scale.

02

design system architecture

Built a large enterprise design system that keeps things consistent across dozens of projects and lets us spin up new ones in record time.

03

research

The whole design team and I use Microsoft Clarity to gather real insights into user behavior. It lets us adjust the UX quickly and see how well our mechanics are performing.

04

b2b panel

A large B2B panel serving 150+ internal users and external teams. I owned the UX and later led the UX team that maintains the panel today. Most screens are now designed or prototyped to ~90% completion with the help of AI.

05

ai integration

Beyond off-the-shelf tools like Claude design, we used custom plugins and programs built with Claude code to push our prototyping further.

 

 

We launched an app for mass-generating prototypes on an infinite canvas, along with various plugins for working with art and other tooling.
