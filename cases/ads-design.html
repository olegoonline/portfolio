ADS Design Studio

Agency-side UI/UX across B2C digital products — landing pages, multi-page websites, and design systems for client projects, plus B2B admin interfaces for internal products.

Web · B2C + B2B / UI/UX Designer / Sep '19 — Oct '20

about

ADS Design Studio was a full-service digital agency taking on B2C and B2B work — landing pages, multi-page websites, design systems, and admin interface design for internal products.

I worked as UI/UX Designer, owning the full process from UX planning and wireframing through final UI and implementation handoff.

challenges

Two distinct tracks: consumer-facing B2C products where visual polish and conversion mattered, and B2B admin interfaces where clarity and information density were the brief.

  • Designed B2C digital products — landing pages, multi-page websites, and design systems from UX planning through final UI for client projects.
  • Designed B2B admin interfaces for internal products — UX flows, component design, and implementation handoff.
  • Owned handoff to front-end — annotated specs, asset exports, and QA on staging.
  • Worked across multiple brand systems back-to-back — adapted visual language fast while keeping internal patterns consistent.
