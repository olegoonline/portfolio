// BRIEF

ADS was a full-service digital agency with a steady mix of e-commerce, corporate sites, and brand microsites. I joined as a UI designer working under art directors on whatever rolled in.

The variety was the point — different industries, different design languages, different stakeholders every few weeks.

// APPROACH

Agency work is a craft school in disguise. The interesting growth was in working fast without sacrificing the details.

  • Took briefs from art directors and turned them into UI for desktop + mobile in tight cycles.
  • Owned hand-off to front-end — annotated specs, asset exports, QA on staging.
  • Iterated on visual style across many brand systems — restraint when needed, swagger when allowed.
  • Built a personal kit of patterns that traveled between projects without dragging the visual identity along.
