// APPROACH

Breadth-first — taking on varied work to learn where the constraints actually come from. Landing pages taught conversion thinking; HTML emails taught the discipline of designing for constraints; social branding taught visual system thinking.

  • Designed landing pages, presentations, and HTML emails for digital products — from brief to production-ready assets.
  • Designed social media branding — visual systems that held up across formats and content types.
  • Hosted webinars on graphic design — structured content and facilitated live sessions for designers and content managers.
  • Prepared training materials — documented process and principles in formats others could act on independently.
