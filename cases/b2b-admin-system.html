B2B admin system

End-to-end UX for a multi-role back-office used by nearly 100 operators — multi-state workflows, content tooling, and role-based cabinets for ops, support, finance, and compliance.

Web · Internal Tool / Lead Product Designer / Dec '24 — Present

about

The back-office is the operational backbone of the platform — handling content, configuration, support, finance, and compliance across all 13 brands. It needed to support 50–100 internal and external operators working concurrently.

I designed the end-to-end UX from the ground up: information architecture, multi-role permissions model, and the visual system that ties it together.

challenges

The hard part wasn't any single screen — it was making one tool serve dramatically different roles without becoming a Swiss Army knife that's worse at everything.

  • Role-based cabinets for ops, support, finance, and compliance — each role sees only the surfaces and data they need.
  • Multi-state workflows with clear status visibility and audit trails — operators always know what stage a record is in and who touched it last.
  • Content tooling that scales to dozens of brand-specific configurations without duplicating UI.
  • Designed dense data screens to stay readable under heavy daily use — error rates dropped despite higher operator throughput.
